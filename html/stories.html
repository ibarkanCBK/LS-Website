Hear From The Village

More Love

Thank you Ancestors, I feel you praying

for my Protection, Affection, Connection and Correction

with all these slayings, for the attention that you’re paying

to what I’m laying down and saying.

I’m in a real life recording booth

channeling the energy of Brother Howard and

Preventing Long Term Anger and Aggression in Youth.

I feel the Prayers of Love, Life, Peace and Hope

as I try to avoid the Ropes of these times

walking on egg shells trying not to be the stats or the crimes

and hearing my grandmother say that one stitch in my time will save nine.

I receive these prayers for all my Brothers

From the Spirit of our Ancestors

and the Lungs of our Fathers and Mothers.

I understand that the world you left as a legacy

is nowhere near what you intended it to be,

but with so much Trauma, Pain and Misery

you have to understand how hard it is for me to see

that the point of your struggle was a “Free Me To Be”.

I’m just trying to survive by staying alive

and sometimes dream of a strive to thrive

and a need to “Be” before I’m forced into a Beehive.

I’m in the Now and right now I have no a Real Blueprint for How

and why I am supposed to Shine like a Super Blue Moon in the clearest African Sky.

Escalating thoughts revealing historical trauma of a past disaster

Has my heart beating faster…. and faster

and the anger of this moment

has muted the words of my Ancestors Pastor.

In the Now can I Calculate, Locate and Communicate,

Deescalate, Pray, Breathe and slow down my Heart rate;

knowing that these next 2 minutes may decide my fate.

Now this moment has become perplexing, Emotions on 10, Body’s under Siege

and thoughts turn to instincts of Fight, Flight or Freeze.

I’m Feeling this in my Head and Chest

the Anger is vexing and the Now has become Next.

No more phone so it’s too late for texting

I’m Breathing and Breathing and feeling the next thing

because the next 16 bars that I’m getting ready to spit

In this recording booth of my life is going to have to be a hit.

So if I can recast the past and recode the mask

I just may have a chance to complete my task

and hear my Ancestors rejoice, echo Brother Dean’s Voice

and make Freedom my Ultimate Choice.

So Keep Praying for my Pathways and that I open the right door

so that this young Brother will continue to PLAAY because you Love Me More

Dr. Bruce Purnell, Executive Director

The Love-More Movement, Inc.

Transformative Life Learners, Leaders & Coaches impacting Cycles & Circles (TLC3’s)
